Cooling Tower Optimization

Minimize power usage and operating costs.

Cooling towers are an integral piece of equipment for many processes, from power generation to food processing. Such systems which utilize fans to create draft (both forced and induced) can use a substantial amount of power, representing higher operating costs or decreased operating efficiency.

Opportunity - Minimize Powe Usage and Maximize Cooling Effect
